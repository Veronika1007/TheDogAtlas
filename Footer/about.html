Built by Dog Parents, for Dog Parents

The story behind TheDogAtlas and our mission to change how we travel with our best friends.

Why We Started

The idea for TheDogAtlas was born out of a simple struggle we faced as dog parents ourselves. Whenever we traveled, we spent hours trying to find dog-friendly activities and places to go. After countless searches, we realized there wasn't a single, fully encompassing website or app that had everything we needed in one place.

"Initially, this started as a fun activity for our own travel itineraries. But the more we thought about it, the more we wanted to bring this to all dog parents out there."

Our Mission

We know the challenges that dog owners face. Our goal is to make life easier and your travels more pleasurable, whether you have one tiny dog, a large breed, or a whole pack. We believe that bringing your dog along should be a joy, not a logistical headache.

For the Community

This is a working project that is very dear to our hearts. We are building more than just a search tool; we are establishing a community where dog parents can support one another. We want to help you create wonderful memories with your lovely dogs, wherever your journey takes you.
